Who Are

Paw Care is a NGO which looks after the betterment of stray and abandoned dogs, through mutual understanding, collaboration and long lasting relationships. We motivate the people in the society to show love and kindess towards the helpless creatures. We encourage and inspire people to join hands towards the fair and humane treatment for the animals across the globe.
